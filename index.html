In Loving Memory

New Addition To Disney Plus!

Hey guys! Did you all see that Dog with a Blog got added to Disney Plus? We can finally see this show after 6 years of wanting to see more! I missed the antics that Stan and the kids had to hide his secret that he could talk. I missed how Stan would swoon over Princess. I also missed seeing Freddy and Gracie. I wanted to see more of their talking journey, but I am still just as excited to see this show again! I think the one thing I missed most as a kid was really watching the family get closer and closer over the entire series. I can't wait to rewatch my childhood again!

In Memory of Kuma, the original Stan

It is with a heavy heart that I tell you all that Kuma, the original dog that played Stan, has passed away. The official Mick Instagram account recently posted about it, and that post will be linked here. I have plans to make a lovely Memorial for him here on the blog so we can all remember him fondly for hooking us on such a fun show! If any of you have suggestions for the Memorial, go ahead and send me an email about it.
